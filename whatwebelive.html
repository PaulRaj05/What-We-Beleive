What We Believe

Bible

The Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

God

One God eternally exists in three persons: Father, Son (Jesus Christ), and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

Jesus is fully God and fully human, born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died for sins, rose bodily, and will return.

Salvation

Salvation comes through faith alone in Jesus Christ, apart from works.

Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit indwells believers, enabling them to live a godly life.

Church

The Church is the body of Christ, comprising all believers, with Jesus as its Head.

Ordinances

Believer's baptism by immersion and the Lord's Supper are observed.

Second Coming

Jesus will return to judge the living and the dead.

Eternal Life

Believers will spend eternity with God in heaven, while unbelievers will face eternal separation.

Separation

Believers are called to live separate lives, distinct from the world.
